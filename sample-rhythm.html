Establish a Vertical Rhythm

Use our .vertical-grid class to add a background that can help establish a vertical rhythm. The line-height of the grid should be adjusted for each project, as well as the vertical offset to align to the first element. 

background-image: url(http://basehold.it/i/31/cc0000);
background-position: 0px 14px;;

Adjust the '31' in the first line to change the space between lines. Look at your rendered line-height on a <p> to see what the value needs to be. The adjust the position offset if needed.
